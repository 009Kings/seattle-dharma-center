About Us

Welcome to the Seattle Dharma Center! We are a group of Buddhist practitioners in the Dzogchen Longchen Nyingtig lineage under the guidance of His Eminance Dzogchen Khenpo Choga Rinpoche. We aim to help all beings achieve lasting happiness by following the Buddha Path as taught by Buddha Shakyamuni. Whether you have experience with Buddha's teachings or are just interested, we offer teachings meant to benefit everyone. For more information on the lineage, visit theBuddhaPath.org.

Schedule

Parking at Epiphany: All the streets around the parish are available for parking, there is also a lot across the street on Howell Ave. From Howell, take the red brick stairs, the garden room is on the left.

Please consider giving $10 donation to help pay for the room rental. Dana (donation) to the teacher is optional and super awesome for visiting teachers; there will be envelopes to put dana in and katas to give it with. The amount you give is not as relevant as the spirit (gratitude/ devotion) in which you give it.
