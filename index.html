About Us

Welcome to the Seattle Dharma Center! We are a group of Buddhist practitioners in the Dzogchen Longchen Nyingtig lineage under the guidance of His Eminance Dzogchen Khenpo Choga Rinpoche. We aim to help all beings achieve lasting happiness by following the Buddha Path as taught by Buddha Shakyamuni. Whether you have experience with Buddha's teachings or are just interested, we offer teachings meant to benefit everyone. For more information on the lineage, visit theBuddhaPath.org.
